About Community CrimeReport

CCRP is a platform that enables quick and easy reporting of crimes. Any type of Crime can be reported on this platform and necessary actions will be taken to solve the crimes.

To report a crime, click on register and sinup for a free account. After registration is complete, click on report crime to login into the system.

You can then report a crime and track the progress of the crime you reported
